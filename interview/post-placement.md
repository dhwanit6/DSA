# Post-Placement - Maintain Your Edge Without Burnout

> Placement is not the finish line. Keep your problem-solving muscle alive with low maintenance.

---

## 1) First 90 Days Plan

1. Solve 1 medium problem per week.
2. Review 1 design topic per week.
3. Keep a short learning log from real work tasks.
4. Practice one communication/debrief note weekly.

---

## 2) Maintenance Routine (Long-Term)

1. Monthly: one mock interview or timed coding session.
2. Quarterly: revisit 10 core DSA patterns.
3. Every 6 months: refresh resume and project narratives.

---

## 3) Worked Example (Busy Work Week)

Problem: heavy sprint load, no time for full prep blocks.

Low-effort plan:

1. Tuesday: 25-minute pattern revision.
2. Thursday: solve one medium with strict 35-minute cap.
3. Saturday: 30-minute retrospective on one production bug.

This keeps continuity with less than 2 hours total.

---

## 4) Common Failure Modes and Fixes

| Failure mode | Why it happens | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Completely stopping after offer | Relief phase + no structure | Keep 1 weekly coding block |
| Only doing work tickets | No interview-style constraints | Add monthly timed problem |
| No story updates for resume | Small wins get forgotten | Weekly achievement log |
| Ignoring system design | Team tasks feel enough | Monthly design recap task |

---

## 5) 30-Minute Weekly Template

1. 10 min: read one old journal entry.
2. 15 min: solve one focused snippet/problem.
3. 5 min: record one learning from work.
