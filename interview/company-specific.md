# Company-Specific Prep

> This is not about brand names. It is about pattern frequency.

---

## 1) Build Your Target List

1. Choose top 5 companies.
2. For each, collect 15-20 tagged questions.
3. Track which patterns repeat.

---

## 2) Prep Loop (Weekly)

1. Solve 5 tagged problems.
2. Record patterns that appear twice.
3. Add one company-specific story for behavioral.

---

## 3) Interview Focus

- Product companies: DSA medium-heavy + clean explanation.
- Larger tech: DSA + behavioral + system design basics.
- Startups: depth on core DSA + tradeoffs.
