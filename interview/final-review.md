# Final Review - The 3-Day Checklist

> The last 72 hours are about clarity and calm, not learning new topics.

---

## 1) 3 Days Before

1. Re-solve 5 weakest problems.
2. Review cheatsheets once.
3. Practice 2 behavioral stories aloud.

---

## 2) 2 Days Before

1. One mock interview only.
2. Review edge cases and templates.
3. Light revision of weak patterns.

---

## 3) 1 Day Before

1. No new problems.
2. Sleep 8 hours.
3. Prepare interview setup and environment.

---

## 4) Interview Morning

1. Quick warm-up: 1 easy problem.
2. Skim notes for 10 minutes.
3. Breathe. Calm pace is your advantage.
