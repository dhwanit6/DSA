# Behavioral Guide - STAR That Actually Works

> You cannot wing behavioral. Prepare it like DSA.

---

## 1) Minimum Story Set (8 Stories)

1. Failure and recovery.
2. Conflict with teammate.
3. Ownership and initiative.
4. Leadership without authority.
5. High-pressure deadline.
6. Learning new tech fast.
7. Impact and results.
8. Ethical decision.

---

## 2) STAR+ Template

1. Situation
2. Task
3. Action
4. Result (with metric)
5. Reflection (what you learned)

---

## 3) Practice Loop

1. 2-minute version.
2. 4-minute version.
3. Record and fix filler words.
