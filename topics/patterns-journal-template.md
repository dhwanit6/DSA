# Patterns Journal Template

---

## 1) Why It Works

Writing patterns makes recall faster and reduces repeated mistakes.

---

## 2) Template (3 Sentences)

1. Pattern: what was it?
2. Trigger: what clue told you?
3. Mistake: what will you avoid next time?

---

## 3) Example Entry

Pattern: Sliding window.
Trigger: "longest substring without repeats".
Mistake: forgot to shrink window in a loop.
