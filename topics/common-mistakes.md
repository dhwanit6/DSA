# Common Mistakes - Avoid These 12

---

## 1) The List

1. Skipping complexity analysis.
2. Solving only easy problems.
3. Jumping to DP too early.
4. No journaling.
5. No mocks until the last week.
6. Copying solutions without understanding.
7. Not talking aloud.
8. Ignoring edge cases.
9. Overfitting to one company.
10. No revision schedule.
11. Changing plans too often.
12. Burnout from zero rest.
